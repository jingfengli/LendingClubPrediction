To fund or not to fund?

Using historical data, I built an app that can predict the outcome of a given loan. This app reduces the chance of funding a failing loan by 50%, compared to approaches used by current Lending Club users.

How to use it?

To predict the outcome of a loan you are considering for investments, enter the loan’s information into the app and hit submit.
You can also run demo predictions using historical data from real loans. Simply click on one of the “demo” buttons below.
